Dropped Out of UC Santa Cruz

Early 2025

Made the difficult decision to drop out of UC Santa Cruz after facing multiple challenges. With a 1.9 GPA putting me on academic probation, losing a job I had held for 2 years (which hit pretty hard), and mounting financial stress from tuition and rent payments, things were getting overwhelming. I was often skipping meals due to financial constraints, and external pressures were piling up. There were days when it all became too much and I broke down. Rather than continue struggling with all of these challenges, I chose to take a leap of faith and focus full-time on my early-stage startup (0mmr at the time), betting on myself.
